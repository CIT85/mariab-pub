Thoughts on using ChatGPT for Research

Chat reminds me of a different website I heard about in high school. We could upload a file and it would just rewrite it using different words inplace of the original, which would then would result in a paper that didn't make sense. I was experimenting with Chat and noticed that is does just spit out generic answers and sometimes in the mix there will be some sections that don't make sense. It is a good way to get ideas out and a lot at once. It's kind of like when you write down any thought to generate ideas. It also helps to have countless of different answers to the same question.
